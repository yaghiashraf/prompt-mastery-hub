The Context Window Trap: More Data != Better Answers

Just because Gemini 1.5 Pro has a 2-million token window doesn't mean you should paste your entire database into it.

The "Lost in the Middle" Phenomenon

Research shows that LLMs pay the most attention to the beginning and the end of the prompt. Information buried in the middle of a massive context dump often gets ignored or "hallucinated away."

RAG Optimization Strategies

Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) is the art of fetching only the relevant context. But prompts play a huge role here.

1. Structured Context Injection

Don't just dump raw text. Use JSON or XML to label the data.


<context>
  <document id="1">...</document>
  <document id="2">...</document>
</context>

2. "Needle in a Haystack" Prompts

Explicitly instruct the model: "Answer using ONLY the provided context. If the answer is not in the context, state 'I do not know'." This reduces confident hallucinations.
